Hong Kong, China

I am Ge Yuan (袁格), now a PhD Student in the Department of Computer Science of The University of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Dong Xu. My research topic lies in the area of Embodied AI, which connects the techniques of Computer Vision and Robotics. From Feb. 2024 to Aug. 2024, I worked as a Research Assistant for Dong Xu in The University of Hong Kong (HKU). I have been served as a Research Engineer on Computer Graphics and Vision in XVerse from Aug. 2023 to Feb. 2024. I got my M.S degree from Computer Science School in Sun Yat-sen University in Jun. 2023, advised by Huicheng Zheng. I received my B.S degree in Computer Science from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I worked as a Research Intern in Tencent AI Lab from Apr. 2022 to Jun. 2023, advised by Maomao Li, Xiaodong Cun, Yong Zhang, Xintao Wang, and Ying Shan.
